ABSTRACT
Watson, named after IBM founder Thomas J. Watson,
was built by a team of IBM researchers who set out to
accomplish a grand challenge––build a computing system
that rivals a human’s ability to answer questions posed in
natural language with speed, accuracy and confidence.
The quiz show Jeopardy! provided the ultimate test of
this technology because the game’s clues involve analyzing subtle meaning, irony, riddles and other complexities
of natural language in which humans excel and computers
traditionally fail. Watson passed its first test on Jeopardy!, beating the show's two greatest champions in a
